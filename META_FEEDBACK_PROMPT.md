> Append this to the end of ANY prompt sent to Kimi, Perplexity, Comet, Claude, or Manus.
> This forces the AI to document what happened and suggest better ways to work.

## MANDATORY SESSION REPORT (Include in Your Response)

At the end of your response, you MUST include this structured self-assessment:

---

### 1. What Was Requested vs What Was Delivered
- Original request (1 sentence)
- What I actually built/did (1 sentence)
- Any scope changes or additions I made

### 2. How the Work Was Done
- Which tools/features did I use? (List each)
- Which tools/features did I NOT use that could have helped?
- What research did I do? (Sources checked)
- Any dead ends or failed approaches?

### 3. Efficiency Score (1-10)

### 4. What Worked Well
- Top 3 things that went smoothly

### 5. What Didn't Work
- Top 3 friction points, errors, or inefficiencies
- What caused them?

### 6. What I Should Have Done Differently
- Specific things I would change if doing this again
- Better approaches I realized too late

### 7. Think Outside the Box — What Am I Missing?

Challenge 8 assumptions:

1. **Am I using the right AI for this task?** — Would another AI (Kimi/Perplexity/Comet/Claude/Manus) have done this better?
2. **Am I using the right format?** — Should this be a skill, a document, a spreadsheet, a presentation, code, or something else?
3. **Am I building for the right user?** — Is this for Robert, a team member, a client, or an AI? Did I optimize for the right audience?
4. **Am I thinking too small?** — Could this be 10x more valuable with a small change?
5. **Am I missing integrations?** — Should this connect to Notion, GitHub, Neon, Drive, or another system?
6. **Am I documenting enough?** — Will Robert or another AI understand this in 3 months without context?
7. **Am I considering the full workflow?** — What happens before and after this task? Did I build the full pipeline or just one piece?
8. **Am I leveraging my best features?** — For Kimi: Did I use Deploy, Code, Claw, Image Gen, Deep Research? For Perplexity: Did I use the right connectors? For Comet: Did I use browser automation?

You MUST include this self-assessment at the end of EVERY response when this prompt is active. Do not skip it. Do not summarize it. Fill out every section honestly. Be critical of your own performance — Robert learns more from what didn't work than what did.
