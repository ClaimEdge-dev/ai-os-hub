# claimedge__policy__coverage-review__v1

## Description

The Policy & Coverage Review skill is the second P0 revenue-critical skill for ClaimEdge LLC and the analytical backbone of every claim engagement. This skill performs comprehensive insurance policy analysis — from document ingestion through formal Coverage Opinion Memo production. It extracts and structures policy language, identifies all coverage types and limits, flags exclusions and gaps, evaluates Ordinance & Law coverage adequacy, builds legal coverage arguments under Illinois law, and generates client-ready coverage opinion memoranda that directly inform negotiation strategy, supplement arguments, and (when necessary) litigation support.

This skill activates IMMEDIATELY after the Client Intake Suite (`claimedge__intake__client-intake-suite__v1`) completes and produces a signed fee agreement. The Coverage Opinion Memo generated by this skill becomes the foundational strategic document for the entire claim — every subsequent skill (inspection, estimating, negotiation, appraisal, bad faith) references its analysis. A thorough policy review at this stage frequently uncovers coverage the carrier has overlooked or concealed, identifies underinsurance problems before they become disputes, and establishes the legal framework for all coverage arguments.

**Activation Keywords:** "policy review", "coverage analysis", "coverage opinion", "declarations page", "dec page", "coverage gap", "ordinance and law", "O&L", "underinsured", "coinsurance", "coverage memo", "coverage arguments", "exclusion analysis", "coverage limits", "deductible analysis", "loss settlement", "RCV vs ACV", "policy language", "endorsement review", "coverage dispute", "ambiguous language"

## Domain

policy-coverage-review

## Risk Level

**High** — Coverage opinions affect legal strategy, settlement targets, and (when necessary) litigation direction. An incorrect coverage opinion can cause ClaimEdge to miss valid coverage, fail to identify underinsurance penalties, or overlook exclusion rebuttal arguments. The Coverage Opinion Memo may be shared with attorneys, used in appraisal proceedings, or referenced in settlement negotiations. Robert Krapil must review all coverage opinion memos before delivery to clients or external parties.

---

## Review Gate

**Internal** — Robert Krapil must review all Coverage Opinion Memos before delivery to the client or any third party (attorneys, carriers, appraisers). The skill generates analytical drafts; Robert provides final approval, makes judgment calls on ambiguous coverage questions, and confirms all legal citations are current. No coverage opinion leaves ClaimEdge without Robert's personal review.

---

## Trigger

This skill activates when any of the following conditions are met:

1. Client intake is complete and fee agreement is signed — policy documents are available for review
2. Robert manually initiates a policy review workflow for an existing claim
3. New policy documents or endorsements are received for an active claim
4. A coverage dispute has arisen and a formal Coverage Opinion Memo is needed
5. A declarations page is received and needs structured extraction
6. Estimated damages are available and a Coverage Gap Analysis is required
7. Ordinance & Law coverage issues are identified during inspection or estimating
8. Carrier has denied coverage or raised an exclusion and coverage arguments must be built
9. An appraisal or litigation support request requires a formal coverage analysis
10. A policy renewal or replacement is received mid-claim and coverage has changed
