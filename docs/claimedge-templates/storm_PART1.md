### 🔍 Damage Types to Inspect For

**Roof System**
- Bruised, cracked, or missing asphalt shingles
- Dented or displaced metal roofing panels
- Damaged ridge vents, pipe boots, or chimney flashing
- Granule loss visible in gutters or downspouts
- Damaged soffit/fascia boards

**Exterior Systems**
- Dented, cracked, or torn vinyl/aluminum siding
